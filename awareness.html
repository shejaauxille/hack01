DEPRESIION

while depression among teenagers in Rwanda is a growing concern, it is also an opportunity for societal change . By raising awareness, providing education, and improving access to mental health services, Rwanda can ensure that its youth receive the support they need to thrive emotionally and mentally.

MAJOR DEPRESSIVE DISORDER (MDD).

A chronic, mild form of depression lasting for at least two years, where symptoms like low mood and lack of motivation persist but are not as severe as in major depression

PERSISTENT DEPRESSIVE DISORDER (PDD)

A chronic, mild form of depression lasting for at least two years, where symptoms like low mood and lack of motivation persist but are not as severe as in major depression

BIPOLAR DISORDER

Depression that occurs at certain times of the year, usually in the winter months, due to reduced sunlight exposure, leading to symptoms like fatigue, irritability, and low mood.

SEASONAL AFFECTIVE DISORDER (SAD)

Depression that occurs at certain times of the year, usually in the winter months, due to reduced sunlight exposure, leading to symptoms like fatigue, irritability, and low mood.
