ENT Services at KMC

Our ENT specialists provide comprehensive care for conditions affecting the ear, nose, and throat. We offer advanced diagnostic and treatment services to ensure the best possible care for your head and neck health. Our services include:

  • Outpatient consultation and follow-up care
  • Inpatient follow-up for ENT patients
  • Non-surgical ENT procedures
  • Removal of foreign bodies from the ear, nose, or throat
  • Ear surgery
  • Head and neck surgery
  • Pediatric ENT services for children
  • Hearing center services (hearing tests and hearing aid fittings)
  • Balance and vertigo clinic for dizziness and related conditions

Laboratory Services at KMC

Our modern laboratory offers fast and accurate diagnostic testing to support timely and precise medical decisions. We ensure your medical results are safe, reliable, and confidential.

  • Hematology Unit: Full Blood Count (FBC), Blood Grouping, and other hematology tests.
  • Hemostasis Unit: Bleeding Time, Clotting Time, and other coagulation tests.
  • Biochemistry Unit: Glucose Level, Kidney Function, Liver Function, Heart Function tests, and more.
  • Serology Unit: HIV Testing, CRP, ASLO, RPR, and other serological examinations.
  • Microbiology Unit: Stool Analysis, Malaria Testing, and other microbiology tests.
  • Rapid Testing Unit: Pregnancy Test, Rubella Test, and other quick diagnostic services.
  • ELISA Unit: Hormone Testing and other ELISA-based diagnostics.
